In this episode of the AI Right podcast, I joined Kris and Andy to explore what really happens when AI moves from concept to production and why scaling GenAI is far more complex than it first appears.

We discussed the foundations of AI systems unpacking what sits underneath the models we use every day. Introducing RISC-V, we discuss how this open and extensible instruction set could play a key role in shaping the future of AI infrastructure.
